# Data sources, tools, and accessions

## Datasets

**Validation set: 10x Genomics 4 donor CD8 pMHC dextramer dataset.**
Per-cell paired TCR contigs and binarized dextramer specificity calls. Files
expected by the pipeline: `donor{1..4}_all_contig_annotations.csv` and
`donor{1..4}_binarized_matrix.csv`. Public 10x Genomics application note dataset
("A New Way of Exploring Immunity"). Not committed (large); place under
`data/dataset/`. Only donor 1 is required to reproduce the validation and
retrieval arms.

**Application set: TABLO repertoire.** Zenodo 10.5281/zenodo.13119615. Large,
not redistributable; not committed.

**Reference for annotation: VDJdb-derived labelled TCR set**, accessed through the
TCR Explorer package. The 10x dextramer study is itself a source for this
reference, which is why the leakage guard is necessary.

## Tools

- **Protenix** (`protenix_base_default_v1.0.0`) for structure prediction, run on
  Colab with an A100 or H100 GPU.
- **ColabFold MMseqs2 API** for the unpaired MSA per protein chain.
- **TCR Explorer** (PyPI `tcr-explorer`) for germline V domain reconstruction,
  TCRdist, and paired similarity search. Installed editable from a local checkout
  during development; the PyPI package is the citable dependency.
- **EBI IPD/IMGT-HLA REST API** for HLA class I heavy chain ectodomains.
- **Biopython** for CIF parsing in QC.
- **Claude Agent SDK** for the multi-agent orchestration layer.

## Sequence constants

- Beta 2 microglobulin mature chain: UniProt P61769, signal peptide (residues 1
  to 20) removed.
- HLA class I ectodomain: first 275 residues from the conserved mature start
  motif (G or C) SHSM (R or K) YF, covering the alpha1 alpha2 alpha3 domains.

## Committed reproducibility artifacts

- `docs/validation_donor1_metrics.json`: the full validation arm output.
- `paper/data/*.csv`: derived tables for every figure and table, regenerated by
  `paper/make_paper_data.py` from the raw folds.
- `runs/*/manifest.json` and `runs/*/benchmark_report.md`: per-run panel
  definitions and scored reports (local; see note below).

## Reproducibility note

The raw Protenix folds (CIFs and per-sample confidence JSONs) live under `runs/`
and are gitignored because they need a GPU to regenerate and, for TABLO, cannot be
redistributed. The paper's figures do not need them: they read the committed
`paper/data/*.csv`. To regenerate those CSVs from raw folds, run
`paper/make_paper_data.py`. To reproduce the validation arm from scratch, run
`scripts/run_validation_arm.py data/dataset out.json 1`.
